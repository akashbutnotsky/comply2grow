What is Import Export Code (IEC)?

Importer Exporter Code (IEC) is a 10-digit identification number issued by DGFT. It is mandatory for businesses involved in importing or exporting goods and services from India.

Why IEC is Essential?

  • Mandatory for international trade
  • Required for customs clearance
  • Necessary for foreign remittances
  • Eligible for export incentives
  • Lifetime validity

Who Can Apply?

  • Proprietorship
  • Partnership Firm
  • LLP
  • Private Limited Company
  • Trust / Society / HUF

Documents Required

  • PAN Card of Business
  • Address Proof of Business
  • Bank Details (Cancelled Cheque)
  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Aadhaar / DSC of Authorized Signatory

Benefits of IEC Registration

  • Access global markets
  • Smooth customs processing
  • Claim export incentives
  • No renewal required
  • Enhances business credibility

Frequently Asked Questions

Is IEC mandatory?

Yes. Any business involved in importing or exporting goods or services must obtain IEC from DGFT.

Does IEC require renewal?

No. IEC has lifetime validity. Annual confirmation on DGFT portal is recommended.

Can individuals apply for IEC?

Yes. Individuals, proprietors, and companies can apply for IEC registration.
