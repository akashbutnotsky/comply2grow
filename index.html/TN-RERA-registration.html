What is RERA Registration?

RERA Registration is mandatory under the Real Estate (Regulation & Development) Act, 2016 for real estate agents and promoters to ensure transparency and buyer protection.

Who Needs RERA Registration?

  • Real Estate Agents & Brokers
  • Property Dealers
  • Real Estate Promoters & Developers
  • Projects exceeding 500 sq. meters
  • Projects with more than 8 apartments

Why is RERA Important?

  • Legal authorization to sell properties
  • Increases buyer confidence
  • Prevents legal disputes
  • Mandatory for advertising projects
  • Ensures regulatory compliance

Validity & Renewal

  • Valid for 5 years
  • Renewal required before expiry
  • Continuous compliance required

Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • Fine up to ₹10,000 per day
  • Up to 5% of project cost
  • Possible imprisonment up to 1 year

Documents Required

  • Identity & Address Proof
  • Business Registration Documents
  • PAN Card
  • Project Details (if promoter)

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RERA registration mandatory?

Yes. Agents and eligible promoters must register before marketing properties.

How long is RERA registration valid?

It is valid for 5 years and must be renewed before expiry.

Can RERA registration be revoked?

Yes. Authorities may revoke registration for non-compliance or unfair practices.
