What is Trademark Registration?

Trademark registration is a legal process that gives you exclusive rights over your brand name, logo, slogan, or symbol. It helps distinguish your business from competitors and protects your identity in the marketplace. Why Trademark is Important?

  • Protects your brand identity legally
  • Prevents others from copying your name/logo
  • Builds trust and credibility
  • Creates long-term business value
  • Essential for startups, MSMEs & online businesses

What Can You Trademark?

  • Brand Name
  • Logo
  • Tagline / Slogan
  • Product Name
  • Packaging Design

Trademark Registration Process

  • Trademark Search (Check availability)
  • Application Filing
  • Examination by Authority
  • Publication in Journal
  • Registration Certificate

Trademark registration in India is governed under the Trade Marks Act, 1999 and requires proper filing and verification before approval. [oai_citation:1‡iPleaders](https://blog.ipleaders.in/trademark-law-in-india/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does trademark registration take?
Usually 6–12 months for full registration, but ™ can be used immediately after filing.

Is trademark mandatory?
Not mandatory, but highly recommended to protect your brand legally.

What is validity?
Trademark is valid for 10 years and can be renewed indefinitely.
