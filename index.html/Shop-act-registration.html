What is Shop & Establishment Act?

Shop and Establishment Registration is a mandatory legal requirement for businesses in India. It regulates working conditions, employee rights, and business operations.

Who Must Register?

  • Retail & wholesale shops
  • Restaurants & cafes
  • Offices & IT companies
  • Online & e-commerce businesses
  • Home-based businesses
  • Warehouses & service providers

Key Provisions Under the Act

  • Working hours & weekly holidays
  • Employee leave & overtime rules
  • Timely wage payment
  • Child labour prohibition
  • Maintenance of employee records

Why is Shop Act Certificate Important?

  • Required for current bank account
  • Needed for GST registration
  • Business loan approvals
  • Proof of business address
  • Legal business recognition

Documents Required

  • Business Address Proof
  • Identity Proof of Owner
  • PAN Card
  • Employee Details (if applicable)

Validity & Renewal

  • Validity varies by state
  • Some states offer lifetime registration
  • Others require renewal every 1–5 years
  • Delay may attract penalties

Is Shop Act registration mandatory?

Yes. Most commercial establishments must register under their respective state Act.

Is it required for online businesses?

Yes. Online and home-based businesses must obtain Shop Act registration.

How soon must I register?
