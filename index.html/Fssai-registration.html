What is FSSAI Registration?

FSSAI Registration is a mandatory license issued by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India under the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. It is required for all individuals and entities involved in food manufacturing, processing, storage, distribution, or sale.

Types of FSSAI License

  • Basic Registration – Turnover below ₹12 lakh
  • State License – Turnover between ₹12 lakh and ₹20 crore
  • Central License – Turnover above ₹20 crore

Who Needs FSSAI Registration?

  • Restaurants & Cafes
  • Cloud Kitchens
  • Food Manufacturers
  • Wholesalers & Distributors
  • Food Importers & Exporters
  • Home-based Food Businesses

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar / PAN)
  • Business Registration Documents
  • Address Proof of Premises
  • Food Safety Management Plan
  • Bank Details

Benefits of FSSAI Registration

  • Legal compliance under FSS Act
  • Improves customer trust
  • Mandatory for online food platforms
  • Required for exports & retail tie-ups
  • Protection from penalties

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FSSAI Registration mandatory?

Yes. All food business operators must obtain FSSAI registration or license before commencing operations.

What is validity of FSSAI License?

FSSAI License is valid for 1 to 5 years depending on the period selected during application.

Can I operate without FSSAI?

No. Operating without FSSAI registration can result in heavy penalties and business shutdown.
