What is Trust Registration?

Trust Registration under the Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950 is required for establishing a charitable or religious trust in Maharashtra.

Who Should Register a Trust?

  • Charitable Organizations
  • Educational Institutions
  • Religious Organizations
  • NGOs planning CSR funding
  • Social welfare groups

Key Benefits

  • Legal Recognition of Trust
  • Eligibility for CSR Funding
  • Eligible for 12A & 80G Registration
  • Improves Credibility
  • Access to Government Grants

Documents Required

  • Trust Deed Draft
  • Address Proof of Trust Office
  • PAN & ID Proof of Trustees
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • No Objection Certificate (if rented premises)

Registration Process

  • Trust Deed Drafting
  • Execution & Notarization
  • Filing with Charity Commissioner
  • Scrutiny & Approval
  • Issuance of Registration Certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

How many trustees are required?

Minimum 2 trustees are required for trust registration in Maharashtra.

Is Trust Registration mandatory?

Yes, if you wish to operate legally and receive funding or donations.

Can Trust apply for 12A & 80G?

Yes. After registration, the Trust can apply for tax exemption benefits.
