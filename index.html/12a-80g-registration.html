What is 12A Registration?

12A registration allows NGOs and charitable organizations to claim exemption from paying income tax on surplus income. Without 12A, your NGO’s income is taxable under normal provisions.

What is 80G Registration?

80G certification allows donors to claim tax deductions on donations made to your organization. This significantly boosts credibility and encourages larger contributions.

Benefits of 12A & 80G

  • Complete income tax exemption
  • Donors receive tax deduction benefits
  • Improves credibility & funding opportunities
  • Required for CSR & large grants
  • Long-term financial sustainability

Documents Required

  • NGO Registration Certificate
  • PAN Card of NGO
  • Trust Deed / MOA
  • Financial Statements
  • Details of Office Bearers
