What is Udyam Registration?

Udyam Registration is an official recognition issued by the Government of India to Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). Once registered, businesses receive a permanent Udyam Registration Number (URN) and an e-certificate.

Key Benefits of Udyam Registration

  • Priority in Government Tenders
  • Collateral-Free Loans under CGTMSE
  • Lower Interest Rates on Loans
  • Protection Against Delayed Payments
  • Subsidies & Government Incentives
  • Trademark & Patent Fee Concessions

Who Can Apply?

  • Proprietorship
  • Partnership Firm
  • Private Limited Company
  • LLP
  • HUF
  • Co-operative Societies

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Number
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Account Details
  • GSTIN (if applicable)
