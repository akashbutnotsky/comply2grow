What is GuideStar Registration?

GuideStar India Registration is a transparency certification for NGOs that validates their legal, financial, and operational credibility. It helps organizations build trust among donors, CSR partners, and funding agencies.

Why is GuideStar Important?

  • Improves credibility among donors
  • Boosts CSR partnership opportunities
  • Required by many funding agencies
  • Demonstrates transparency & accountability
  • Enhances NGO public profile

Who Can Apply?

  • Registered Trusts
  • Registered Societies
  • Section 8 Companies
  • NGOs with valid legal registration

Documents Required

  • NGO Registration Certificate
  • PAN Card of NGO
  • 12A / 80G (if available)
  • Financial Statements
  • Details of Governing Members
