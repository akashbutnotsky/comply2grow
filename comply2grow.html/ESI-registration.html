What is ESI Registration?

Employee State Insurance (ESI) registration is governed by ESIC under the ESI Act, 1948. It provides medical and social security benefits to eligible employees.

Who Must Register?

  • Establishments with 10+ employees (in applicable areas)
  • Retail outlets & commercial shops
  • Hotels & restaurants
  • Hospitals & educational institutions
  • Factories & service establishments

Eligibility Criteria

  • Employees earning up to ₹21,000/month
  • ₹25,000/month for disabled employees
  • Business meets employee threshold

Benefits of ESI Registration

  • Medical benefits for employees & families
  • Maternity & sickness benefits
  • Disability compensation
  • Dependents’ benefits
  • Funeral expense coverage

Documents Required

  • Business Registration Certificate
  • GST Registration (if applicable)
  • PAN of Business & Employees
  • Address Proof
  • Employee Details & Salary Records
  • Cancelled Cheque

Post-Registration Compliance

  • Monthly contribution payment
  • Wage register maintenance
  • Attendance register
  • Accident register
  • Timely return filing

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ESI Registration mandatory?

Yes. Mandatory for establishments meeting employee and wage criteria.

What is the employee wage limit?

₹21,000 per month (₹25,000 for disabled employees).

Are there penalties for non-compliance?

Yes. Non-registration or late contributions may attract penalties under ESIC rules.
