What is PF (EPF) Registration?

Provident Fund (PF) Registration is the process through which an establishment registers with the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) under the EPF Act.

When is PF Registration Mandatory?

  • Establishments employing 20 or more employees
  • Includes permanent, temporary & contractual workers
  • Register within 1 month of reaching threshold
  • Voluntary registration allowed

EPF Contribution Structure

  • Employee – 12% of basic salary
  • Employer – 12% of basic salary
  • 3.67% EPF account
  • 8.33% Pension Fund (EPS)

Benefits of PF Registration

  • Retirement savings
  • Pension benefits
  • Insurance under EDLI
  • Improves employee trust
  • Labour law compliance

Documents Required

  • Business Registration Certificate
  • PAN of Business & Directors
  • Address Proof
  • Cancelled Cheque
  • Employee Details
  • Digital Signature Certificate

Post-Registration Compliance

  • Monthly PF payments
  • PF return filing
  • Wage record maintenance
  • Employee KYC updates

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PF Registration mandatory?

Yes. Mandatory for establishments employing 20 or more employees.

Can small businesses register voluntarily?

Yes. Businesses with fewer than 20 employees may register voluntarily.

What is an EPF Code?

A unique establishment number issued by EPFO after registration.
