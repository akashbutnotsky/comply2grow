How We Work

01

Document Collection & Eligibility Review

If you are an NGO seeking funding, documents are everything.

You must have:

  • Registration Documents
  • Income Tax Documents
  • Financial Statements
  • Annual Reports
  • CSR Related Compliance Documents

We take detailed eligibility review.

02

Detailed Eligibility Report

You receive a structured report stating:

  • Whether you are eligible for CSR funding
  • What is stopping you
  • What corrections are required
03

Two Possible Outcomes

Option A – Ineligible NGO

We guide you step-by-step on how to become eligible in the near future.

Option B – Eligible but Documentation Gaps

  • Compliance corrections
  • Required documentation
  • CSR ready documentation pack

(Professional charges applicable)
