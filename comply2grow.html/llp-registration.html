What is an LLP?

LLP combines partnership flexibility with limited liability protection. It has a separate legal identity and protects partners’ personal assets.

Key Features

  • Separate legal entity
  • Minimum 2 partners required
  • No maximum partner limit
  • Limited liability protection
  • Lower compliance than Pvt Ltd

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 2 partners
  • 1 Indian resident designated partner
  • DPIN required
  • Capital contribution as agreed

Documents Required

  • PAN & ID proof of partners
  • Address proof
  • Registered office proof
  • Digital Signature Certificate
  • LLP Agreement draft

Registration Process

  • Obtain DSC
  • Apply for DPIN
  • Name Reservation
  • File FiLLiP Form
  • Draft & File LLP Agreement
  • Receive Incorporation Certificate

Advantages of LLP

  • Limited liability
  • Flexible management
  • Lower compliance burden
  • Cost-effective structure
