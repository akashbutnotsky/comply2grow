What is a Private Limited Company?

A Private Limited Company is a legally recognized business entity under the Companies Act, 2013. It offers limited liability protection, separate legal identity, and strong credibility for growth and funding.

Key Benefits

  • Limited liability protection
  • Separate legal entity status
  • Easy to raise funding
  • Perpetual succession
  • Higher credibility with investors

Minimum Requirements

  • Minimum 2 Directors
  • Minimum 2 Shareholders
  • Registered Office Address
  • Unique Company Name

Documents Required

  • PAN & Aadhaar of Directors
  • Passport size photos
  • Address proof of directors
  • Office address proof
  • Utility bill / Rent agreement

Our Registration Process

  • Name approval from MCA
  • DIN & DSC generation
  • MOA & AOA drafting
  • Company incorporation filing
  • Certificate of Incorporation issuance

Post-Incorporation Compliance

  • Opening bank account
  • Appointment of auditor
  • Annual ROC filing
  • Income Tax compliance
