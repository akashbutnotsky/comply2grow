What is Professional Tax?

Professional Tax is a state-level tax levied on income earned through employment, trade, profession, or business. Maximum annual limit is ₹2,500.

Who Must Register?

  • Employers hiring staff
  • Self-employed professionals
  • Companies, LLPs, Firms, HUFs
  • Doctors, Lawyers, CAs
  • Freelancers & Consultants

Why Registration is Mandatory?

  • Mandatory state compliance
  • Required for payroll processing
  • Avoid penalties & interest
  • Required for labour registrations

Employer Responsibilities

  • Deduct PT from employee salary
  • Deposit tax with state government
  • File returns on time
  • Maintain compliance records

Due Dates

  • 20+ employees → Monthly payment
  • Below 20 → Quarterly payment
  • Return filing as per state rules

Documents Required

  • Certificate of Incorporation / LLP Agreement
  • MOA & AOA
  • PAN Card of Entity
  • Business Address Proof
  • Director/Proprietor ID Proof
  • Employee Salary Details

Penalties for Non-Compliance

  • Late Registration – Daily penalties
  • Late Filing – Monetary fines
  • Late Payment – Interest & penalty

Benefits of Professional Tax Registration

  • Smooth compliance process
  • Avoid legal risks
  • Legal business operations
  • Claim tax deduction on PT paid

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Professional Tax applicable in all states?

No. It is applicable only in certain states like Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, etc.

What is PTRC & PTEC?

PTRC is for employers deducting PT, PTEC is for professionals paying their own tax.

What is the maximum Professional Tax amount?

The maximum limit is ₹2,500 per year.
